## 0:00 - 0:15 Intro

Hi, this is People Photo Prompt Maker US, a guided prompt builder for US small businesses that need realistic people-centered photos for hiring, local marketing, and consultation requests.

## 0:15 - 0:40 Problem

Small businesses often need trustworthy people photos for flyers, Facebook posts, Instagram posts, and hiring campaigns. But writing a good image prompt is hard because the owner has to decide the audience, subject, scene, photo style, aspect ratio, and AI-specific constraints.

This tool turns that into a guided workflow.

## 0:40 - 1:25 Product Walkthrough

First, I choose the business goal, such as increasing job applications or increasing customer inquiries.

Then I choose the channel, the AI tool, and the region. The app is built around US small business use cases.

Next, I choose an industry preset. For example, real estate agency automatically sets the audience to local families, the subject to an agent helping a family, and the scene to a home living room.

The prompt updates live on the right.

## 1:25 - 1:55 Details And AI Constraints

I can open the details panel to control people count, relationship, person detail, scene, framing, and light.

The tool also handles generator-specific background choices. In ChatGPT mode, I can request a transparent-background cutout. In Gemini or Google Flow mode, the tool switches to a solid-background instruction, which is easier for those generators to control.

The prompt explicitly says not to include text, logos, signs, trademarks, watermarks, or UI elements inside the image, because those should be added later in Canva or another design tool.

## 1:55 - 2:30 How Codex And GPT-5.6 Were Used

Codex and GPT-5.6 were used as the main product-building partner. They helped narrow the concept from a broad prompt maker into a focused tool for US small business people photos.

Codex helped design the workflow around business outcomes, write the US-focused presets, implement the standalone HTML, CSS, and JavaScript app, add generator-aware background behavior, and prepare the README and Devpost submission materials.

## 2:30 - 2:45 Closing

The project is a single static HTML file with no setup or dependencies. Judges can open it directly in a browser, test the controls, and copy a prompt immediately.
